FOAG Ordinance
on Organic Farming

of 11 November 2020 (Status as of 15 March 2022)

The Federal Office for Agriculture (FOAG),

based on Articles 23 para. 1, 23a para. 2 and 24 para. 6 of the organic farming ordinance of 22 September 19971,

ordains:

1 SR 910.18

Art. 1 List of countries

Organic products from countries listed in Annex 1 with relevant specifications may be marketed with the labelling relating to organic farming.

Art. 2 List of recognised certification bodies and inspection authorities outside the list of countries

The certification bodies and inspection authorities recognised under Article 23a paragraph 2 of the Organic Farming Ordinance of 22 September 1997 are listed in Annex 2.

Art. 3 Commencement

This Ordinance comes into force on 1 January 2021.

Annex 12

2 Revised by No I of the FOAG O of 6 May 2021 (AS 2021 272) und of 10 Feb. 2022, in force since 15 March 2022 (AS 2022 92).
